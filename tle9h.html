The Pandemic

What Is Covid?

COvid-19 is a disease by a new strain of coronavirus. 'CO' stands for
corona, 'VI' for virus, and 'D' for disease. Formerly, this disease was
reffered to as '2019 novel coronavirus' or '2019-nCoV

Symptoms:

Fever
Dry Cough
Lost of Sense of Smell
Lost of Sense of Taste

How does the virus spread?

The virus that causess Covid-19 is mainly transmitted through droplets generated when and infected person cough, sneeze, or exhale.
These droplets are too heavy to hang in the air, and quickly fall on floor or other surface

Safety Protocols

-Clean your hands often.
-Cough or Sneeze in your bent elbow not in your hands
-Avoid touching your eyes, nose and mouth
-Limit social gatherings and time spent in crowded places
-Avoid close contacts with someone who is sick
-Clean and disenfect frequently touched objects and surfaces
-Always wear mask and face shields
